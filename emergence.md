### Requested modules

**1. Data and measurement foundations**

1. **Company brain** — the intelligence layer: an entity knowledge graph across people / vendors / customers / deals / operating divisions at Emergence, serving as a composable surface for building metrics and automations.
2. **AI pilling** — driving up AI usage across the org, with the goal of measuring usage and correlations with team outcomes (a measurement question, not just a spend report).

**2. Engineering platform**

1. **Foundations to assess quality & health** — ability to spin up architecture / pipeline audits with costed modernization plans; shared component library.
2. **Cloud spend consolidation** — consolidate multicloud billing, redundancy detection, volume discount modeling, routing, etc. (client can share their tech DD reports to refine this).
3. **Tech stack analysis and unification** — build a universal reference stack and container standards so architecture is recognizable across products.

**3. Automation family**

1. **Automation platform** — lets technical and non-technical people build / test / deploy AI agents and automations against a reliable standard, with integrations to all their tools.
2. **Competency automation layers** — a data layer under an agent runtime, built per function.
3. **AI coworker** — custom-built for non-technical staff (similar to Claude Cowork but custom-built to interact with all their tools).

**4. Applied automations**

1. **Automated integration management** — execution of their 90-day post-acquisition playbook (info gathering, admin access provisioning, migrations, etc.).
2. **Customer health and churn** — build a list of customer success products; client will share more as they land on what's best for each company.
